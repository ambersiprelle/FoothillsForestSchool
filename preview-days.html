Spring 2026 Preview Days

Preview Days

Come join us for a preview day and see what Foothills Forest School is all about!

Preview day is our version of open house. We will explain a little more about our Forest School and do our best to convey the magic that happens when learning and development is not constricted by four walls and fluorescent lights. You will have the opportunity to tour the camp, meet staff and other potential families and learn all about our goals for this program.

Dress for the weather and we will see you in the forest!

Preview Days are being offered January 24, February 8 and March 7 and May 3.

* Enrollment for the 2026-27 school year will open on February 1, 2026 for prospective families.

*Sign-ups are managed through Brightwheel. You will be prompted to create an account to register.
