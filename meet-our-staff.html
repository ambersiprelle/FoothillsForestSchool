Meet Our Staff

Our team of passionate outdoor educators is committed to every child's growth and wellbeing.

Our Team

Qualified. Committed. Passionate.

Every educator at Foothills Forest School is dedicated to providing quality education outdoors. Our staff see each child as an individual and honor their unique learning pace — never rushing development or comparing children to one another.

We bring a combination of formal education credentials, outdoor education training, and genuine love for working with children in nature.

What We Believe

How Our Team Approaches Every Child

  • Each child is a unique individual deserving individual attention and respect
  • Learning should never be rushed — every child develops on their own timeline
  • Children are capable of far more than we typically expect
  • The role of the educator is to guide, inspire, and support — not to dictate
  • Nature is the best classroom for developing the whole child
  • Families and educators are partners in every child's education
