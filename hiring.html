We're Hiring

Join our team of passionate outdoor educators in the foothills of the Smokies.

Join Our Team

Work Where the Classroom is the Forest

At Foothills Forest School, we are always looking for educators who share our passion for outdoor learning and child development. If you believe that nature is the best classroom and that every child deserves to be seen as an individual — we want to hear from you.

We are a growing school with a strong community of families, a beautiful campus at Montvale Springs, and a deeply held commitment to doing right by every child we serve.

Ideal Candidates

What We Look For

🌿

Love of Nature

You genuinely enjoy spending time outdoors in all weather conditions and can inspire that love in children.

❤️

Child-Centered Mindset

You see each child as a unique individual, honor their pace, and believe deeply in child-led learning.

📚

Relevant Experience

Background in early childhood education, outdoor education, Montessori, or a related field. Certifications a plus.

🤝

Team Player

You communicate well, support your colleagues, and embrace the collaborative nature of our small school community.

Open Roles

Current Openings

Our staffing needs evolve with our growing programs. We typically hire for:

  • Lead Forest Educators — for preschool and enrichment programs
  • Classroom Assistants — supporting our lead teachers in all programs
  • Summer Camp Counselors — seasonal positions each June and July
  • Family Friday Facilitators — part-time, monthly sessions
To apply: Email your resume and a brief note about why you're drawn to outdoor education to Info@FoothillsForestSchool.com. We'll be in touch!
