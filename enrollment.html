How It Works

6-Step Enrollment Process

We keep our enrollment process simple and transparent. Here's what to expect:

Review Materials

Explore this website and download the Parent Handbook to fully understand our program and expectations.

Submit Application

Complete your application through Brightwheel, our school management platform. Create a free account to get started.

Application Review

We review all applications within 2 weeks and will be in touch to discuss fit and availability.

Enrollment Documents

Once accepted, complete all required enrollment documents through Brightwheel.

Pay Enrollment Fee

Pay the non-refundable $100 enrollment fee to secure your child's spot.

Pay First Tuition

Pay your first month's tuition by the stated deadline to finalize enrollment.

Common Questions

Enrollment FAQ

Do I need to attend a Preview Day before applying?

It's not required to apply, but Preview Day families receive priority enrollment for the following school year. We strongly encourage attending to ensure our program is the right fit.

What are the age requirements?

Forest Preschool requires children to be at least 2.5 years old and toilet proficient. Forest K/1 requires children to be 5 (for Kindergarten) or 6 (for 1st Grade) by August 15th.

Is the enrollment fee refundable?

No — the $100 enrollment fee is non-refundable. It secures your child's spot in the program once all documents are complete.

What platform do you use for enrollment?

We use Brightwheel for all enrollment paperwork and tuition payments. You'll need to create a free Brightwheel account to apply.

What should children wear?

Children should dress for the weather — we are outdoors in all conditions! Layers, rain gear, and appropriate footwear are important. We'll share a detailed packing list upon enrollment.
