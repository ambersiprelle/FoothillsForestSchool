Enrollment Process

Welcome to Foothills Forest School

1. Review Materials

Explore our website and read our parent handbook to ensure that you have a thorough understanding of our forest school approach to education.

*For preschool enrollment, students must be toilet proficient and at least 2.5 years old by their first day of school.

2. Submit Application

Submit the Application form below. The Application Form is managed through Brightwheel. You will be prompted to create an account to complete the forms.

3. Application Review

Applications will be reviewed within two weeks. If space is not available in the applied-for program, students are placed on a waiting list.

4. Enrollment Paperwork

Once application is approved, complete enrollment paperwork shared via Brightwheel.

5. Pay Enrollment Fee

Pay non-refundable enrollment fee of $100.

6. First Tuition Payment

Make first tuition payment by tuition deadline.
