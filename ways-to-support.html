Our Project

The Building Fund

Foothills Forest School is based at the beautiful historic Montvale Springs property in Maryville, TN. We have two active building projects underway:

  • Historic Cabin Renovation: Transforming a beautiful old cabin on the property into a dedicated schoolhouse for our programs
  • New Enrichment Building: Constructing an additional structure specifically designed for our Homeschool Enrichment classes

These permanent spaces will give our students safe, comfortable places to gather during weather events, store materials, and expand what we're able to offer.
