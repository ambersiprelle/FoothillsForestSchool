Family Fridays at Foothills Forest School

Family Fridays

Bring the whole family to enjoy a morning in the forest

Family Fridays is an opportunity to enjoy some of the magic of forest school as a family. We will enjoy a morning of modified forest school rhythms, participate in themed activities and crafts, and allow families some free time to play and explore our camps.

Each session will be from 9AM -12PM.

This is open to all age children + their adult(s).

Cost is $50/family.

Family Friday Details:

February 13th Session

"Love-Birds"

March 27th Session

Fire Skills

April 24th Session

Animal Tracking

*Sign-ups are managed through Brightwheel. You will be prompted to create an account to register.
