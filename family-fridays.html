Family Fridays

Monthly outdoor learning sessions for the whole family — children of all ages welcome with accompanying adults.

A Community Gathering

Learning Together as a Family

Family Fridays are our monthly community events where families come together to experience nature-based learning firsthand. These sessions are designed for children of all ages accompanied by a parent or guardian.

Each month focuses on a different topic — from fire skills to animal tracking — giving your family hands-on experience with the kinds of activities we practice in our regular programs.

Schedule9 AM – 12 PM (half-day sessions)
Cost$50 per family per session
AgesChildren of all ages with an accompanying adult
LocationMontvale Springs, Maryville, TN

Come Prepared

What to Bring

  • Weather-appropriate clothing and footwear (we're always outdoors!)
  • Rain gear if rain is in the forecast
  • Water bottle and a snack for each family member
  • An open mind and a spirit of adventure
  • Your curiosity — nature has a lot to teach us!
