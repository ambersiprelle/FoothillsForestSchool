Summer 2026

A Summer in the Forest

Foothills Forest School Summer Camp brings the magic of our year-round programs to summer. Each week features a unique theme that guides our nature exploration, crafts, games, and learning adventures.

Campers are grouped by age for developmentally appropriate activities, and every week ends with new skills, new friends, and unforgettable memories made in the forest.

2026 Schedule

Weekly Themes

Each week of camp has its own theme, guiding activities, explorations, and projects throughout the week.

Week 1 • June 1–5

🌱 Plant Power

Explore the plant world — from tiny seeds to towering trees. Learn to identify local plants and discover how they support the ecosystem.

Week 2 • June 8–12

☀️ Sensational Summer

Celebrate all that summer has to offer through our senses — sight, sound, smell, touch, and taste in the natural world.

Week 3 • June 15–19

🌦️ Weather Wonder

Investigate weather patterns, clouds, rain, wind, and the science behind what shapes our summer days.

Week 4 • June 22–26

🏕️ Wilderness Adventure

Navigation, shelter-building, fire safety, and wilderness skills for young adventurers ready to explore beyond the trail.

Week 5 • June 29 – July 3

🐦 Songbirds of the Smokies

Discover the birds that call the Smokies home. Learn to identify calls, behavior, and habitats of our local avian neighbors.

Week 6 • July 7–11

🏔️ Appalachia Week

Celebrate the rich culture, history, and natural heritage of Appalachia through stories, crafts, music, and forest exploration.

Week 7 • July 14–17

🦋 Animal Olympics

How fast, strong, or clever are animals compared to us? Friendly competitions inspired by the amazing abilities of the animal kingdom.
