Our Philosophy

An Outdoor Classroom Rooted in Play

Foothills Forest School creates an open-ended space that allows for development, with an approach rooted in play and tailored to individual student needs. Our outdoor classroom model aligns educational objectives with children's natural curiosity about the world around them.

We are proud to diversify educational options in our community while empowering students to develop self-awareness and understand their impact on the world. Every child is seen as an individual — we honor their unique learning pace and style.

What We Believe

Three Pillars of Our Approach

Every program we offer is built on these foundational values.

🌲

Pillar One

Outdoor Education

Time in nature promotes a balance of cognitive development, emotional resilience, and social skills. We develop motor coordination, spatial awareness, and fine/gross motor abilities through forest exploration.

👩‍🏫

Pillar Two

Our Team

Qualified educators committed to providing quality education outdoors. Our staff see each child as an individual and honor their unique learning pace, never rushing development.

🎨

Pillar Three

Play-Based Learning

Children develop motor skills through climbing and balancing, foster curiosity through nature observation, and build early scientific thinking — all through meaningful play.
