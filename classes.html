Program One

Forest Preschool

Our Forest Preschool offers a rich, nature-based learning environment for children ages 3–5. Mixed-age groups allow older children to model skills while younger children are challenged and inspired.

Children must be toilet proficient and at least 2.5 years old to enroll. Dress for the weather — we are outdoors in all conditions!

Ages3–5 years (mixed age groups)
ScheduleMon & Wed OR Tue & Thu, 9 AM – 2 PM
Class SizeUp to 12 students with 2 teachers
Tuition$350/month
RequirementsToilet proficient; at least 2.5 years old

Program Two

Forest K/1

Our Forest Kindergarten and 1st Grade program provides a full-day outdoor education experience taught by a state-certified teacher. This program is ideal for families seeking an alternative to traditional school that still meets academic standards.

Students engage in structured learning through nature-based projects, outdoor investigations, and hands-on exploration aligned to grade-level objectives.

AgesKindergarten (5 by Aug 15) and 1st Grade (6 by Aug 15)
ScheduleMon–Thu, 9 AM – 2 PM
Class SizeUp to 15 students
Staff1 certified teacher + 1 assistant
Tuition$950/month
