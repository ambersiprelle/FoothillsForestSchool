Outdoor Gear for Forest School

We are outdoors almost all of our time, every day — rain or shine, cold or hot. To keep your child comfortable and safe, please make sure they arrive to the forest with appropriate clothing and outdoor gear for the weather each day.

The lists below serve as a guideline as you prepare for the year. When in doubt, overpack — extra layers can always be added or taken off as the day warms or cools.

Every Day, All Seasons

Essentials

  • Outdoor clothing that's okay to get dirty — long pants are recommended to help prevent injury and bug bites
  • No dresses or skirts without shorts underneath
  • Closed-toed shoes (no sandals or flip-flops)
  • Non-spill water bottle (water only)
  • A daypack with all items labeled and a full change of clothes

By Season

Seasonal Gear Lists

Summer

  • Sun hat
  • Sunscreen
  • Bug spray
  • Bathing suit
  • Water shoes
  • Towel
  • Cold lunch with an ice pack

Fall & Spring

  • Long sleeve base layer
  • Warm jacket
  • Fleece
  • Wool socks (no ankle socks)
  • Waterproof hiking shoes or BOGS boots

Winter

  • Base layers
  • Weatherproof jacket with a hood
  • Snow pants
  • BOGS or snow boots
  • Waterproof mittens
  • Neck gaiter
  • Warm hat that covers the ears

We're Here to Help

Need Gear?

If you are unable to provide required items for your child, please speak with our director. We keep a variety of extra gear on hand for families in need — no child should miss out on the forest for lack of a warm coat or a pair of boots.
