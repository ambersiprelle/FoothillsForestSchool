Ways to Support Foothills Forest School

At Foothills Forest School, learning takes root outdoors — through muddy boots, curious questions, and moments of wonder shared beneath the trees. As our programs continue to grow, we are lovingly working toward spaces that can better shelter, support, and inspire our students year-round.

Your support helps us take two meaningful next steps:

  • Renovating a historic cabin at Montvale Springs into a welcoming schoolhouse
  • Building an additional structure to serve our homeschool enrichment classes.

These spaces will allow us to deepen our connection to place while expanding access to nature-based education for more children and families.

There are many ways to walk alongside us in this season of growth—whether by:

  • wearing and sharing our t-shirt fundraiser
  • Making a direct donation through cash, check, or Venmo (@FoothillsForestSchoolTN)
  • Supporting our classes through our Amazon Wishlist

Every contribution, big or small, helps us nurture a learning environment where children can grow with confidence, care for the land, and feel a true sense of belonging.

Thank you for being part of our community and for helping Foothills Forest School continue to flourish.
